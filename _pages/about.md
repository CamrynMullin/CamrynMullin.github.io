About Me
======
I was born and raised in Montreal, QC, Canada, though my parents are from Nova Scotia. I have always been facinated with astronomy, and other planets specifically, and it brings me great joy to live in a place where I can see more stars. In my free time, I enjoy figure skating (both watching and partaking), hiking, cycling and rock-climbing.  


My Research
======
I am a PhD Candidate at the University of Victoria, where I did my masters in 2022-2024. I study planet formation through near-inrared observations of protoplanetary disks. I work with both space-based and ground based imaging, and a variety of high-contrast imaging techniques such as ADI, PDI and RDI. My relevant publications and talks can be found in the tabs above, and should be up-to-date, though you can check my google scholar for the most reliable information. I welcome you on this journey with me as we try to understand the formation and evolution of planets.
